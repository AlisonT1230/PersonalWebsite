About Me

My name is Alison and I'm a computer science specialist student at U of T. I found my love for computer science when I was in high school, taking my first course on the subject where I greatly enjoyed developing the final project with a team of friends. I've always loved learning how things work and using that knowledge in my own applications.

In my time outside of academics, I like to work on personal projects to expand my skillset outside of what the university offers. This includes creating games, experimenting with new tools, practicing photography and exploring graphic design. Additionally, I am part of the Dāna Project developer team, the Innis College InSIGHT mentorship team, and I hold a position of Ads and Publicity Director at Innis Residence.

I would like to learn about and work with a wide variety of computer science topics, including web development, mobile development and game design. That being said, I am always excited to take on new challenges in other areas of computer science and broaden my desire to learn.
