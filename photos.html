Photography

Previous Next

During one summer, I decided to pick up my mom's camera and try my hand at photography. After walking around the park and taking pictures of swings and trees, I grew bored of following the general rules of photography and started pointing my camera at whatever I found interesting. Being an animal person (and more specifically, a bird person), what I found most intriguing was the wildlife around me so I started collecting photos of animals and learning how to identify them.

Now, I take my camera outside as often as I can because I always find something new. It doesn't matter if I'm going to the same few parks every single day because there will always be something that I haven't seen before.

Smaller wildlife tends to be overlooked and underappreciated, but these animals really do have unique personalities and it's incredibly fulfilling to catch them showing their true colours.
